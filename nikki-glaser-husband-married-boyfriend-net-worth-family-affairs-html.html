Is SiriusXMs Host Nikki Glaser Married? Husband, Relationship Talks &amp; Family Details

Publish date: 2024-02-26

Having previously been a part of multiple failed relationships, Nikki Glaser knew how to make relationships work. Drawing on her past experiences, the 34-year-old comedian made sure that other couples would not have to face the ordeal she had to go through, by providing relationship advice on a variety of matters.

American stand-up comedian, host, and producer Nikki Glaser is best known for movies like Trainweck (2015), Punching the Clown (2009) and Inside Amy Schumer(2013). Currently, she hosts SiriusXM’s You Up With Nikki Glaser which airs from 10am-12 EST on Channel 95. 

She has hosted the Comedy Central television series Not Safe With Nikki Glaser, a sex comedy talk show.

She is also recognized for her MTV talk show Nikki & Sara Live, as well as for her appearance in the stand-up comedy themed documentary film I Am Comic (2010).

Nikki Glaser's Bio: From An Actress To A Comedian 

Nikki began her career at the age of 18 when she started performing stand up comedy. From 2011 to 2014, Nikki hosted a podcast with comedian Sara Schaefer naming You Had To Be There. In March 2015, Glaser appeared on Giant Bombs life advice and co-hosted the podcast We Know Nothing with comedian Phil Hanley and musician Anya Marina.

In the same year, she hosted a show named Not Safe with Nikki Glaser which premiered on 9 February 2016. On 5 September 2016, Nikki appeared on the Comedy Central Roast of Rob Lowe. From January to October 2016, she hosted the companion podcast to her show Not Safe with Nikki Glaser. With that in February 2018, she began hosting You Up? with Nikki Glaser. With that, she has also appeared on MTV shows such as Money from Strangers, Awakard After Show, You're Welcome and Failosophy.

With a fruitful 2018, Nikki has already appeared in two American comedy series called A.P Bio and Alone Together. She also appeared in The Comedy Central Roast and as a judge, on the show, Jeff Ross Presents Roast Battle.

Nikki Glaser's Bio: From Family To Networth 

Nikki as of 2018 has been able to collect a net worth of $2 million from her comedy, hosting, and podcast. All the success brought by her career has helped her enjoy a handsome net worth and double-figure salary.

She was born to father EJ Glaser and mother Julie E  on 1 June 1984 in Ohio, United States. She is the only child in her family.

According to Nikki, her father was her biggest supporter motivating her to pursue her dreams. She spent most of her childhood in St. Louis, Missouri. She attended Kirkwood High School and graduated in English Literature from the University of Kansas.

Nikki Glaser Now Advices Married Couples
