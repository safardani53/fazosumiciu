Who is YouTuber Jackie Aina?

Publish date: 2024-09-30

JACKIE AINA is a YouTuber, lifestyle content creator, and an ambassador for change in the beauty community.

She boasts millions of followers across her social media platforms, including 3.5 million subscribers on YouTube.

Who is Jackie Aina?

Jackie Aina is a beauty YouTuber, with over 1.8 million Instagram followers.

You can follow her @jackieaina.

In August 2020, Jackie also created a lifestyle brand called FORVR Mood, in which she sells candles and room sprays.

And she also has a lavishlyjackie channel on Instagram which she said was a "different vibe that doesn’t have to be for everyone of course, but it’s carefree, fun, and a side of me I never get to share for fear of hearing the same stereotypes and microagressions my peers often do.".

Jackie has collaborated with some of the biggest beauty brands in the world, including Sephora, Anastasia Beverley Hills, and Two Faced.

She has strived to encourage diversity across the beauty industry, and partnered with Two Faced to expand their foundation to include darker tones.

What does Jackie Aina do on YouTube?

Jackie posts a range of beauty content to her YouTube channel, as well as life hacks and advice.

She posts make-up routines, makeup reviews, and skincare advice videos.

Some of her most popular videos are the ones where she gives her honest opinion on popular beauty brands and their products.

What did Jackie Aina do before becoming an influencer?

Before Jackie started her YouTube channel in 2009, she had been a student at California State University San Bernardino for two years.

Jackie studied medicine at college, before deciding that it was not the right career path for her.

After dropping out of her medical degree, Jackie underwent a huge change of direction and enrolled in the army.

She joined the Military in 2008 and was placed in the Military reserves.
