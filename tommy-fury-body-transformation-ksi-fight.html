TOMMY FURY has whipped himself into sensational shape for his fight with KSI.

But it is obvious Fury had no problem back in fighting shape as he prepares to come down in weight to face KSI.

Despite his stunning transformation, KSI, 30, hit back: "Will make it so much funnier when I knock him out."
