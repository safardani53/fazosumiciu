Publish date: 2024-10-09

THERE'S only one thing better than a Greggs sausage roll - and that's homemade ones.

But if you struggle to get the pastry to puff up in the oven - then there's another way to do it and it's so much easier.

But one foodie has revealed how you can make them at home in 12 minutes using just three ingredients in your air fryer.

Becky Excell, from the UK, shared her recipe on TikTok and it went viral with over 250k views.

She said: "I must admit, I love a big sausage roll but Greggs just isn't for me, so I make my own," and revealed the best way to make them is in the air fryer.

All you need to make them is sausages, puff pastry, and an egg.

She first starts by de-skinning the sausages to get the sausage meat out and rolls the pastry out and cuts it in half.

Becky then adds a small amount of mustard to the pastry, she uses gluten-free but any will do and explained mustard was optional.

"In less than 15 minutes you can have fresh, homemade sausage rolls," she added.

After spreading the mustard, the foodie adds the sausage meat on top and glazes one side of the pastry before folding it over.

She then crimps the edges with a fork to make sure the pastry stays closed while cooking and cuts slashes on top of the sausage roll to make sure no filling spills out.

The foodie then gives them one final glaze with the egg before adding them to the air fryer for 12 - 14 minutes at 200 degrees.

Becky added: "Another thing I really like about making these in the air fryer is that any excess fat drips through the basket so they're never soggy."

She then showed off the finished sausage rolls which looked pretty similar to the ones you buy in Greggs, and people couldn't wait to try them.

One wrote: "I've done it, brilliant, thank you for showing me how to do it."

Another person commented: "Just got an air fryer.. gonna give these a go."

A third penned: "I’ve just done these. Super easy and came out great. Thanks!"
