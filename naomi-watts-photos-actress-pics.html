Naomi Watts is one of the most talented actresses in Hollywood. See her best pics here, in honor of her birthday.

Naomi Watts is considered by many to be one of the most decorated and accomplished actresses of our time. The England-born beauty rose to fame in the 2001 film ‘Mulholland Drive’ which later led to her first Oscar nomination for her role in ‘21 Grams’ in 2003 alongside Sean Penn and Benicio Del Toro.

It was 9 more years until she received another Oscar nod for ‘The Impossible’ which also earned Naomi her first Golden Globe Award nomination. Other big movies she received praise from critics and fans for included ‘King Kong’ and ‘Birdman or (The Unexpected Virtue of Ignorance)’.

Naomi’s personal life has also made news over the years. She began dating actor Liev Schreiber in 2005 where the two had two sons during their decade long relationship. They never married and eventually split up in 2016.
