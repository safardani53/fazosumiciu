Meggin Patricia Cabot is an American novelist. She has written and published over 50 dramas for teens and adults, and is best known for her adult series Princess Diaries, later adapted by Walt Disney Pictures into two feature films. Cabot has received numerous book awards including New York Public Library Teenagers Books, the American Library Association's Quick Book Award for Reluctant Readers, the Tennessee Volunteer TASL Book, Sense Pick Book, Evergreen Youth Book Awards, the IRA / CBC Youth Choice and many more. She also has the New York Times number one bestseller, and more than 25 million copies of her book are printed worldwide.
