In his fourth time playing for Stamford at the end of the 2012-13 season, he scored the decisive goal in the Premier League Division One South North play-off final. Miller went on to reap his second success with Boston United and was named the Best Player of the North Conference 2013-14 season after scoring 24 goals in 40 league appearances.
