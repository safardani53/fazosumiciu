Snooki Posts Baby Bump Picture in Pajamas, Goes Without Makeup

Publish date: 2024-08-16

No more Club Karma for this Jersey Shore alum! Pregnant Nicole “Snooki” Polizzi joked that her partying days are over while posting a new picture of her baby bump via Instagram on Saturday, July 5.

“Goin hard this 4th of July weekend….#momlife,” the 26-year-old wrote. The fresh-faced star went without makeup in the snapshot, and held onto a bottle of milk for her son Lorenzo, 22 months. She wore a fitted top and leopard pajama pants.

“Can’t believe I’m meeting my daughter in about 2 months!!” she added. “This pregnancy flew by!!!!”

Despite staying in, the reality star did branch out to attend a wedding with fiance Jionni LaValle on the Fourth of July. “My soon to be husband !!! At a July 4th wedding,” she gushed Friday. In a sweet snapshot, Snooki posed with her husband-to-be and covered up her growing belly in a long-sleeve black and white dress.
