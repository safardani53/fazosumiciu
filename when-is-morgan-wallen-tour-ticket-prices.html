Is Morgan Wallen going on tour?

Publish date: 2024-07-14

AFTER injuring his vocal cords, country singer Morgan Wallen was forced to postpone part of his 2023 tour.

However, Morgan Wallen has announced new rescheduled dates — here's what to know.

What 2023 dates were rescheduled for Morgan Wallen's tour?

Due to Wallen's vocal injury, the country singer canceled six weeks of tour dates.

The new rescheduled dates are set for mid-2024.

These dates include:

  • May 18 — Hershey, PA @ Hersheypark Stadium NOW MAY 9, 2024
  • May 19 — East Rutherford, NJ @ MetLife Stadium NOW MAY 17, 2024
  • May 20 — East Rutherford, NJ @ MetLife Stadium NOW MAY 18, 2024
  • May 24 — Austin, TX @ Moody Center NEW DATE TBD
  • May 26 — Houston, TX @ Minute Maid Park NOW NOV. 18, 2023
  • June 1 — Atlanta, GA @ Truist Park NOW NOV. 10, 2023
  • June 2 — Atlanta, GA @ Truist Park NOW NOV. 11, 2023
  • June 3 — Panama City Beach, FL @ Gulf Cost Jam CANCELED
  • June 8 — Virginia Beach, VA @ Veterans United Home Loans Amphitheater at Virginia Beach NOW JUNE 6, 2024
  • June 9 — Virginia Beach, VA @ Veterans United Home Loans Amphitheater at Virginia Beach NOW JUNE 7, 2024
  • June 10 — Myrtle Beach, SC @ Carolina Country Music Fest CANCELED 
  • June 14 — Pittsburgh, PA @ PNC Park NOW AUG. 30, 2023
  • June 15 — Pittsburgh, PA @ PNC Park NOW AUG. 31, 2023
  • June 17 — Philadelphia, PA @ Citizens Bank Park NEW DATE TBD

How can I get tickets to Morgan Wallen's tour?

Though only 14 dates were either rescheduled or canceled, the rest of Wallen's tour is still happening and will resume on July 6, 2023.

Fans can buy tickets to the rest of his One Night At a Time Tour through his website.

Most tour dates will redirect to Ticketmaster, where fans can purchase tickets.

Some tickets will redirect users to the venue that the concert is taking place in.
