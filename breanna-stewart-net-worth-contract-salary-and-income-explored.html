Breanna Stewart Net Worth: Contract, Salary And Income Explored

Publish date: 2024-04-09

Breanna Mackenzie Stewart is an American professional basketball player currently playing for the New York Liberty in the Women’s National Basketball Association (WNBA).

Stewart has a net worth of $1 million as of January 2024.

One of the highest-earning WNBA players, Stewart, signed with the New York Liberty in free agency on February 1, 2023, on a one-year $175,000 contract.

In 2023, she will earn a base salary of $175,000 and an incentive bonus of $5,000 while carrying a cap hit of $180,000.

Her last deal with the Seattle Storm was a one-year contract worth $228,094.

Before this, she was in a two-year $375,500 deal with the team with an average annual salary of $187,750.

Aside from her regular WNBA earnings, Stewart earns well from her shoe deal with Puma. 

On May 8, 2023, the WNBA All-Star and Puma officially unveiled the Stewie 2. This came after the enormous success of Stewart’s first signature shoe.

WNBA Season With Liberty

On Tuesday, Stewie made a highly anticipated return to Climate Pledge Arena as her current team Liberty went head-to-head against her former team Seattle Storm.

This marked her first time visiting the arena as a competitor, a significant moment for the talented basketball player.

Earlier this year, she made headlines when she decided to join the Liberty as a free agent.

This move meant leaving behind the city of Seattle, which had been her WNBA home since she entered the league in 2016.

The game served as a poignant reminder of her remarkable journey and the new chapter in her career.

In recognition of her remarkable contributions, Stewart has been honored with the title of Eastern Conference Player of the Week for the games played from Friday, May 19, to Sunday, May 28.

Breanna Stewart Net Worth & Shoe Deal

As mentioned before, Stewie has a net worth of approximately $1 million as of 2024.

She is one of the highest-paid WNBA players in the nation, earning a six-figure salary each year.

Along with her regular WNBA income, Stewart has a shoe deal with Puma contributing the lion’s share to her net worth.

Breanna released her first signature shoe with Puma, “Stewie 1,” in October 2020.

After the massive success of the first one, in May 2023, her second signature shoe, “Stewie 2,” was released to celebrate the different areas of Stewart’s life that drive her to be a force of nature.

In a touching tribute to the influence of motherhood in her life, Stewart’s signature shoe debuted in the ‘Ruby’ colorway.

Stewart named this particular colorway after her daughter, giving it significant meaning.
