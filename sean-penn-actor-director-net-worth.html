SEAN Penn is an actor, author, director, screenwriter, and producer.

He has been active in the entertainment industry since the early 1980s.

What is Sean Penn's net worth?

Penn is worth $70million according to Celebrity Net Worth.

Born in Santa Monica, California, on August 17, 1960, his father Leo Penn was an actor and director, and his mother Eileen Ryan was an actress.

Penn made his film debut in the 1981 action-drama film "Taps", portraying a military high school cadet. 

Since, he has starred in movies like "Racing with the Moon" (1984), "The Falcon and the Snowman" (1985), "At Close Range" (1986), and the widely-panned "Shanghai Surprise" (1985).

He has won two Academy Awards, for his roles in the films "Mystic River (2003) and "Milk" (2008). 

Penn's debut novel "Bob Honey Who Just Do Stuff" was published by Atria Books in March 2018.
