Who are Michael Jai White's children?

Publish date: 2024-06-03

ACTOR Michael Jai White has revealed that his oldest son died from coronavirus earlier this year.

The 53-year-old told of his grief after losing his firstborn to the deadly disease.

Who are Michael Jai White's children?

Oldest son

Michael Jai White's oldest son passed away from coronavirus "just a few months ago" at age 38.

He fathered his first child when he was just 15-years-old and told of his sadness at his loss because they had "grown up together".

Michael's said his son, who he did not name, lived in a "street element" and was gripped by substance abuse.

"Unfortunately, he was still kinda out there in the streets hustling, wasn’t doing well, started getting on substances. He’d come out, go back in, all that type of stuff."

He explained that his son was not vaccinated against Covid and that his immune system was "compromised" due to his habits, which the Black Dynamite star said was the "knockout blow".

His son is survived by his six children.

Morgan Michelle White

Michael has a daughter, Morgan Michelle White, from his previous marriage to Courtney Chatham.

She was born on December 24, 2008, and he gushed that he was "in love like you would never believe" after the arrival of his first baby girl.

Little is known about the 12-year-old after her parents divorced in 2011.

Jai Jordan White

The Brooklyn-born martial arts expert also has a son named Jai Jordan White who was born in 1995.

He has his father's middle name and was born during a previous relationship, like his sibling Devin White, who was born in 1990.

Jai also prefers to keep out of the spotlight and in his case, behind the camera instead of in front of it like his dad.

He reportedly worked in the Camera and Electrical department for the 2016 short film, A Wish for Mr Whiskers. 

Other children

Michael is the proud biological father to two other children and several others who are not his, thanks to his blended family.

He even once joked that he "needed a moment" to remember how many children he has.

The Spawn star has been married to Gillian Iliana Waters since 2015, who has two daughters - Alaia and Niahla Waters - from her previous marriage to Antonio Brown.
