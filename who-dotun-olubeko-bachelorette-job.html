What does Dotun Olubeko do for a living?

Publish date: 2024-06-27

EVER year The Bachelorette sees a batch of hopeful men all vying to be the last guy standing, and ultimately win the heart of a beautiful woman.

Well one person who knows that feeling well, is 2023 winner Dotun Olubeko.

Who is Dotun Olubeko?

Born on August 22, 1992, Dotun Olubeko hails from Brooklyn, New York, and describes himself as "open-minded, full of love, and the best listener."

He's a graduate of both California State University, Chico, and the University of Western States; where he studied nutritional sciences.

Although he resides in Brooklyn, he and his family are originally from Nigeria.

Speaking about his childhood, Dotun told The Bachelorette viewers: "We came here with nothing, but my parents took on whatever job they could.

"They've done really well to give me a life that I'm happy to live so it's been a really humbling experience."

When was Dotun Olubeko on The Bachelorette?

In the summer of 2023, Dotun shot to fame when he appeared on The Bachelorette's 20th season,

The series started on June 26, 2023, and ended August 21, 2023.

Standing at six foot seven, Dotun was also the tallest of Charity Lawson's competitors.

He was a fan favourite throughout the season, and he was ultimately picked by the show's leading lady - making him the 2023 winner.

At the site of the proposal site she said: "You made me feel alive. You made me believe in love again. You made me feel so valued and so seen.

"I see you as my husband. I see you as my future. I see it so clear and I don't have any doubts. I love you so much."

Dotun then echoed this by saying: "You showed me that a good thing really can be just a good thing. The love that we have is perfect."

Meanwhile, the second runner-up was fellow contestant Joey Graziadei.

What does Dotun Olubeko do for a living?

Dotun is an integrative medicine specialist.

He also owns DPT Fitness; an online health and wellness service.

However, after appearing on The Bachelorette he can also add reality star to his resume.

Thanks to being on the show, Dotun is now an Influencer, and (as of August 2023) has 43K followers on Instagram.
