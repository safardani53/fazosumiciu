Pixie Lott shows some real cat-titude in sexy kitten outfit at Halloween party in LA

Publish date: 2024-08-03

PIXIE Lott showed that she had some real 'cat-titude' as she braved a chilly Halloween in a skimpy kitten outfit.

The Mama Do singer, 25, looked stunning in a low-cut green leopard print body and shorts as she arivved at exclusive LA hotel Chateau Marmont for their annual Halloween party.

The sexy feline went for some simple face paint with some whiskers brushed across her cheeks and a little black triangle on her nose.

Her eyes stood out and were rimmed with black charcoal and had painted her lips a dark red.

She left her golden blonde hair loose and wild and accessorised with cat eared headband and a bowtie.

Pixie showed off her long legs in a pair of over-the-knee black boots and had slung a small handbag over her body.
