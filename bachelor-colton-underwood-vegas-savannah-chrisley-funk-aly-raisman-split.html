Bachelor Colton Underwood admits to Vegas date with Savannah Chrisley while he was 'in a funk' after

Publish date: 2024-03-28

THE Bachelor’s Colton Underwood confirmed he flew to Las Vegas for a weekend date with Savannah Chrisley after being in a “funk” following his breakup with Aly Raisman.

The 28-year-old reality star revealed he was briefly seeing the Chrisley Knows Best personality after his public split from the Olympic gymnast. 

Colton appeared on Bachelor blogger Reality Steve’s podcast for a revealing interview where he confessed to having a “good time” with 23-year-old Savannah.

The former NFL hopeful opened up about seeing the blonde reality star when he was struggling after his Summer 2017 breakup from the gold medalist.

“After dating Aly, a couple weeks went by and I was sort of in a funk and didn’t know if I was going to put myself out there again,” he told the franchise spoiler. 

The Colorado native continued: “Then her and I start talking on social media. And next thing you know I’m in Vegas at the ACM’s. 

"I was very clear, I was like I’m down to meet. I’m down to have a good time. I just got out of a serious relationship and I don’t want to walk the carpet. 

“I don’t want to be photographed together. But I’ll come meet you and your friends.”

Colton said he was so adamant about not being seen by fans or photographers, he took a separate car with the family patriarch to sneak inside the event. 

“I rode in a suburban with Todd Chrisley to the red carpet just to go in through the back door,” the athlete added. 

He then said that his time with Savannah was “more of a low-key, hanging out and having a good time.”

“But I had a good weekend. That is an amazing family and good people,” the star gushed. 

Savannah went on to become engaged to boyfriend Nic Kerdiles - before ultimately calling it off after two years.

At the time of their brief time together, Savannah had already appeared on her TV show for years while Colton had yet to be a household name. 

However, years before he would first appear on Becca Kufrin’s season of The Bachelorette in 2018, he made a splash on a viral social media video in 2016 asking out his ex Aly.

The pair dated from 2016-2017, and he revealed in his memoir that was released earlier this year that he was “broke” while in the relationship.

Before he went on The Bachelor and received his ABC salary - which is reported to be at least $100,000 for the lead - he revealed he had no money despite being thrusted into the Hollywood limelight with his famous girlfriend.

He wrote in the book The First Time: “I was perfectly happy in this relationship. It was satisfying and serious. But I was keeping a secret from Aly. Though I looked like I belonged in this glitzy, glamorous world, I was broke.”

The former NFL hopeful said he lost “most of his savings” after he had to pay out of pocket for a $40,000 shoulder surgery due to a football injury.
