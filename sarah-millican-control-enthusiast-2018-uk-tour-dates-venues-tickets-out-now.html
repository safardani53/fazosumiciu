Sarah Millican 2018 UK tour - Control Enthusiast dates, venues, ticket prices and info

Publish date: 2024-04-17

COMEDIAN Sarah Millican has started an incredibly busy year with her giant new stand-up show.

We have all the dates and venues that you can see the funnywoman on stage…

What is Sarah Millican’s 2018 live tour called?

Sarah Millican has begun an epic new stand up show called Control Enthusiast.

The 42-year-old English comedian kicked off her tour on Saturday 13 January - and won’t be done until Thursday 6 December.

Announcing the tour in June last year, she said: "Do you arrange the nights out? And are you in charge of passports on holiday? If so, then you’re a control enthusiast too!"

She promised anecdotes about “rescuing men, farting in hospital pants, and what can happen at a bra fitting”.
