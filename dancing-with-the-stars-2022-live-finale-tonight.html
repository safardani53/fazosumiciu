Wayne Brady held back tears on GMA

The Good Morning America hosts surprised Wayne Brady with a sweet video from his 19-year-old daughter Maile on live TV.

On Tuesday morning, the four DWTS finalists appeared on the morning show, where they sat down for interviews, sang with Jordin Sparks, danced, and freestyled.

During the interview portion, Michael Strahan told Wayne that they had a "special message for him" from his daughter.

Wayne's partner, Witney Carson, said "aww" as he appeared shocked.

Maile appeared via video and showered her dad with love, saying: "Hey dad. I am so proud of you for working as hard as you did, balancing everything, and doing the show. You are always a winner. And I'm just so excited to spend time with you now that it's over."

The camera flashed back to Wayne, who shut his eyes and smiled before saying, "That's my heart. That's my support system."

"She's the one that every day when I came home and I was like, 'Baby, I don't know if I can do it. My knees. My back.' And she would just hug me and say, 'You can,' so I did."
