Ben Stiller is a beloved actor and director, known for roles in films like ‘Zoolander’ and ‘Meet The Parents.’ Check out photos of him here.

Ben Stiller has been one of the most popular actors in comedy for over 30 years. The son of actors Jerry Stiller and Anne Meara, Ben got his start in the entertainment business at a young age, and he got his breakthrough in 1992, when he starred in the sitcom ‘The Ben Stiller Show,’ which won him an Emmy Award. Since then, he’s gone on to star in some of the most popular comedies of the 90s and 21st Century. He’s starred and produced in blockbuster hits like ‘Zoolander’, ‘Tropic Thunder’, and much more. He’s also worked alongside tons of major stars like Robert De Niro in ‘Meet The Parents’ and Adam Sandler in tons of different pictures.

Ben has been married to his wife Christine Taylor since 2000, and the pair have a daughter Ella, and a son Quinlin together. Scroll through our gallery to check out photos of Ben throughout his career.
