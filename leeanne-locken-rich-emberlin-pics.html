LeeAnne Locken &amp; Rich Emberlin Pics Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-06-09

LeeAnne Locken at the NBC Universal Summer Press Day in Los Angeles in 2016. The reality star wore a flattering white jumpsuit with a gold-colored belt around the waist. 

LeeAnne is best known for appearing as a cast member on ‘Real Housewives of Dallas’ on Bravo. She first started out as a model after moving from her hometown in Houston, Texas to Chicago. She then moved to Los Angeles to pursue acting and took part in many small roles in film and television before joining ‘RHOD’ at the start of its first season in 2016, which was her breakthrough in the spotlight. She has lived in Dallas since before the start of the popular reality series and married her boyfriend of 10 years, Rich Emberlin in a romantic ceremony at the Cathedral of Hope in Dallas on Apr. 27. It was her first marriage and she gained a stepdaughter, Elise, through it.
