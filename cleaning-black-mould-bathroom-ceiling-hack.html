Cleaning fan shares 1 product that will banish mould from your ceiling - it'll be gone in seconds

Publish date: 2024-06-12

A CLEANING whizz has shared their secret to getting rid of horrid black mould from your bathroom ceiling.

The genius hack will banish the grotty cultures in seconds - and it will cost you just a quid.

In a video uploaded to social media the cleaner grabs a bottle of Flash multi-purpose spray bleach.

The grime buster then squirts the foam directly onto the mould spores across the white ceiling.

After a few sprays the cleaner then grabs a mop with a flat sponge.

They then begin scrubbing the ceiling and within seconds the mould wipes clean off.
