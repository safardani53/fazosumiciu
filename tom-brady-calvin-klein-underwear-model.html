Tom Brady: Calvin Klein Underwear Model?

Publish date: 2024-05-23

The Boston Globe, citing a report in In Touch Weekly, is reporting that New England Patriots quarterback and certified hunk Tom Brady is being wooed to become the latest celebrity to model for – wait for it – Calvin Klein underwear.

The publication says the football star has been offered $1 million or more to be a CK underwear stud following in the footsteps of others such as Boston’s own Mark Wahlberg, whose stint served him (and the ladies) very well.

Tom Brady, who had limited his endorsement deals in past years, is suddenly pitching everything from watches (Movado) to bottled water (Glaceau).
