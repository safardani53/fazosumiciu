A SAVVY supermarket shopper has jumped on the trend of checking out Marks & Spencer’s latest bargains.

And it wasn’t long before the account, which can be found at @costoflivingcrisistips, zoomed in on something on the shelves. 

It was a multi-pack of biscuits from M&S own brand that included three different types for just £1.
