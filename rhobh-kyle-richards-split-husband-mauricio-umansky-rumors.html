RHOBH star Kyle Richards has fans wondering if she split from her husband Mauricio Umansky based on a new post.

In the clip, Kyle filmed herself dancing in the dimly lit venue with her daughter, Farrah, 34, each sporting their best party attire.

The duo turned their attention to the camera as they lip-sang the track "Dancing On My Own" by Calum Scott that played in the background.

In another video, her daughter Sophia, 23, showed off her dance moves while donning a sultry skintight black dress with a large cutout by her midsection.

She wore her brunette locks straight down and held a sparkly silver handbag and a cocktail in one hand.

This isn't the first time Kyle and Mauricio's relationship status has been in question.

SPLIT RUMORS

Last month, the couple sparked rumors that they called it quits after fans noticed Kyle ditched her wedding ring during an outing.

Photos obtained exclusively by The U.S. Sun showed the 54-year-old picking up food in Studio City, California.

She donned workout clothes, suggesting that she either had been at the gym or intended to go.

The Bravo star held her phone and a bag of food in her hands but clearly was missing her wedding band.

Speculation about Kyle and Mauricio's marital status has been rampant among fans for some time.

They have addressed the rumors in the past, maintaining that there is little to no truth to reports that their marriage is in trouble.

In October 2022, the Real Housewives star addressed rumors that she had split from her husband and was seeing someone new.

The buzz came after she posted a photo of herself and E! News host Justin Sylvester sitting together, looking lovingly at one another.

The TV star captioned the post: "For better, for worse, for richer, for poorer, in sickness and in health.... #BFFS."

Fans took the post literally and were convinced the pair were a couple.

She took to her Instagram Story in 2022, writing: "Gotta love the internet, attaching an Instagram Story from an article that read in part: "Fans wonder if Kyle Richards has a new husband after star shares sweet pic with BFF Justin Sylvester."

The drama came after Mauricio opened up about his marriage to Kyle on the Side Piece With Melissa Pfeister podcast, saying: "[I] let her talk, let her vent, let her talk about it, listen.

"You know, that's one of the most important things is to actually listen. I think her biggest frustration is when I am tuned out, and she wants to talk about it, and I have my mind on business and other stuff...That's hard."

He went on to suggest that things like that often lead to divorce, adding: "They get to see themselves on television, and they get to see the relationship of what it is themselves, and a lot of people have a perception of their own relationship being different than what it is, and then they see it because they watch it and then that causes the divorce."
