Briana Jungwirth Biography

Publish date: 2024-08-03

Briana Jungwirth is an American personal stylist who recently served in the California fashion shoots. She is also known as the ex-girlfriend of One Direction band member Louis Tomlinson. The pair shares a child; son Freddie Reign Tomlinson together.

Early Life & Career

Briana Jungwirth was born on January 26, 1992, in California, USA, under the birth sign Aquarius. Her mother, Tammi Clark, is a former bikini model and actress while her father, Joe Jungwirth, is a construction manager.

Her parents got split when she was only ten years old. Following the divorce, her dad got married to singer-songwriter, Karen Lawrence.

She was raised along with her brother Austin and stepbrother, Brett Clark (from her mother's side). Briana is of American nationality and belongs to white ethnicity.

As for her education, she has attended Agoura High School from where she completed her graduation in 2010.

Briana is an American personal stylist by profession. She rose to fame after she started dating One Direction band member, Louis Tomlinson.

Personal Life

Briana Jungwirth seems to be currently single and not dating anyone. However, as we mentioned earlier, she was in a relationship with One Direction member Louis Tomlinson.

The former couple first met through a mutual friend in 2015 and started dating shortly after Louis called it quits with his long-time girlfriend, Eleanor Calder.

On January 21, 2016, the pair welcomed a baby boy who they named Freddie Reign Tomlinson. Later in July, conflicts between Briana and Louis started after she noticed that he had a son with another woman, Dannielle Campbell.

After finding that Louis cheated on her, Briana stopped him from seeing Freddie. But later, they did a temporary settlement in which he permitted him to see his son without overnight stay permission. Though all this, the duo separated later on.

In 2016, Briana confirmed her relationship with band member Jayk Purdy by sharing picture and video of her family while on a dinner. However, they parted ways just one month later.

Salary & Net Worth

Both salary and net worth of Briana Jungwirth has not been disclosed. But according to online sources, she has the average salary of a personal stylist in the United States is around $15.35 per hour.

Following her split with Louis, she had received approx. $15 million as child expenses. She also receives $1500 as a monthly allowance.

Briana is currently living with a $100 thousand family condo.
