Augustus Prew - Pure Genius Cast Member

Publish date: 2024-01-30

Hometown: London
Birthday: September 17

Augustus Prew began his career in entertainment when he nabbed a coveted spot with the prestigious YoungBlood Theatre Company in England, which counts British actors Carey Mulligan and Imogen Poots as its alumni.

Prew’s first television role was in the British series “24Seven.” His additional British television credits include guest-starring roles in “The Time of Your Life,” “Silent Witness,” “MI-5,” “The Bill,” “The Village” and “Spooks,” and the television movie “Marigold.”

Prew gained attention from American television audiences when he starred as Prince Alfonso in the Showtime series “The Borgias.” His additional U.S. television credits include guest starring roles in the series NCIS, on the Network, “Major Crimes” and the mini-series “Klondike.” In 2017, Prew will appear in a recurring role in the limited event series, “Prison Break.”

On the big screen, Prew made his debut in the hit movie “About a Boy.” His additional film credits include “High-Rise,” “Kick-Ass 2,” “Copperhead,” “Charlie St. Cloud,” “The Secret of Moonacre,” “Animals,” “Hated,” “The Kid” and “Sophie & Sheba.”

Prew is a native of London, England. Currently, he resides in Los Angeles. His birthday is Sept. 17. Follow him on Twitter and Instagram @augustusprew.
