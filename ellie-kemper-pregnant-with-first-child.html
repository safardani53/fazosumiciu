Ellie Kemper: Pregnant with First Child!

Publish date: 2024-02-25

The second season of Unbreakable Kimmy Schmidt hits Netflix this Friday, April 15.

Somehow, though, this release is only the second most exciting piece of news connected to star Ellie Kemper…

… because she’s pregnant with her first child!!!!!!!!!

The Unbreakable Kimmy Schmidt lead and husband Michael Koman (a writer who previously worked for Conan O’Brien) are expecting to be blessed by a baby at some point this year.

The two dated for a few years before tying the knot in July of 2012 and Kemper made this announcement during a taping today of The Tonight Show with Jimmy Fallon.

It’s unclear at the moment just when Kemper is due, but she was reportedly pregnant while filming Season 2 of her Netflix comedy, so she’s likely into her second trimester by now.

In somewhat related news, Unbreakable Kimmy Schmidt is downright hilarious.

Hurry up and binge watch the first season before this weekend… and then binge watch the second season. You won’t regret it.

We send our very best wishes to Kember and Koman!
