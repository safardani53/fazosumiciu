Ciaras Stunning Wedding Dress: All the Details!

Publish date: 2024-05-03

It’s all in the details! Ciara and Seattle Seahawks quarterback Russell Wilson tied the knot in a romantic ceremony in England on Wednesday, July 6, and the bride and groom marked their moment in high style.

“The bride wore a custom-made Cavalli Couture long-sleeve gown with a deep V neckline, composed of white tulle, fully embroidered with floral motifs throughout and delicate glass beading, covered entirely with precious Chantilly lace appliqué,” a source told Us of the 30-year-old singer’s stunning gown.

Wilson similarly brought his A-game for the ceremony, decked out in a Giorgio Armani tuxedo, classic white wing collar shirt and bow tie.

The 27-year-old excitedly shared a photo on Instagram of the pair walking down the aisle postceremony with the caption: “We are The Wilsons!” Ciara posted a matching photo to the social media platform.

A source tells Us that the lovebirds, who got engaged in March 2016, said “I do” before more than 100 close family and friends, with a bridal party that included Kelly Rowland, Serena Williams and La La Anthony.

The night prior to their big day, they were toasted by loved ones at a prewedding dinner, where Ciara wore a stunning white floor-length halter neck gown and Wilson donned black.

The pair have previously made headlines for being adamant about remaining celibate until marriage. (They began dating in April 2015.)

“I said to her, and she completely agreed: ‘Could we love each other without that?’ If you can really love somebody without that, then you can really love somebody,” the athlete said during an appearance at Rock Church in San Diego in July 2015. “For me, I knew that God had brought me into her life to bless her and for her to bless me and to bless so many people with the impact that she has, that I have.”

“We’re not gonna be perfect, by any means,” he continued. “Life’s not perfect. Nobody’s perfect, but he’s anointed both of us, and I know that he’s calling for us to do something miraculous, something special.”

This is Wilson’s second marriage, and Ciara was previously engaged to the father of her son, Future Jr.
