Strictly: It Takes Two viewers seriously distracted by Johannes' daring outfit

Publish date: 2024-02-08

BBC Strictly Come Dancing fans were seriously distracted by Johannes' "art teacher" outfit on It Takes Two.

The dancer never fails to wow everyone with his bold choices of outfits.

During his appearance on It Takes Two last night, Johanne's wardrobe choice certainly got BBC viewers talking.

The professional dancer - who is partnered up with comedian Ellie Taylor - stunned fans in an oversized white shirt, paired with pinstriped print trousers and a brightly coloured, patterned handkerchief around his neck.

He finished off his look with bright orange-tinted sunglasses that really made him standout.

Fans of the show felt Johanne looked more like an "art teacher" than a professional dancer.

Taking to Twitter to share their thoughts, one fan wrote: "Loving the cool art-teacher vibes of Johannes' outfit today."

Another chimed in: "I can't decide if Johannes is going for the artist-in-smock look, or Katherine Hepburn."

A third added: "Is it just me or does he look like an art teacher or something?"

While a fourth pointed out: "Is anyone seeing this? Why does he look like look he's trying to be some sort of painter?"
