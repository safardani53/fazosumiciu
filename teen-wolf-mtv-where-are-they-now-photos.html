‘Teen Wolf’ is a fan favorite. See what stars Tyler Posey and more are up to now.

Tons of people tuned in to follow Teen Wolf when it aired on MTV from 2011 to 2017. Based on an 80s horror-comedy starring Michael J. Fox, the story was primed for a modern reboot. Fans loved the coming-of-age saga, which followed high school werewolf Scott McCall (Tyler Posey) and the supernaturally gifted teens of Beacon Hills. Most of the cast returned for Teen Wolf: The Movie, which premiered on Paramount+ on Jan. 26, 2023.
