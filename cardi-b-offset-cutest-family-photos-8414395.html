Cardi B and Offset's Cutest Cutest Photos with Their Kids

Publish date: 2024-06-03

Cardi B and Offset are two proud parents.

The pair — whose break up was confirmed in Dec. 2023 after five years of marriage — share two children together: daughter Kulture Kiari Cephus, 5, and son Wave Set Cephus, 2. The Migos rapper is also father to sons Jordan and Kody Cephus as well as daughter Kalea Marie Cephus, from previous relationships.

Cardi B and Offset have opened up in the past about their blended family with the "Bodak Yellow" rapper sharing her desire to bond with Offset's children after they got married.

"After we got married, that's when I first met his kids," she told Essence. "I always wanted them to love me, and they really do. They tell me that. I feel it, and I hope that we always have a great relationship."

From celebrating milestones to special onstage moments, these are some of the sweetest family photos of Offset and Cardi with their children.

Party Time

Cardi and her little girl were matching mermaids in celebration of Kulture's fourth birthday in 2022. And while the boys kept their looks more understated for the mermaid-themed celebration, the family was an adorable foursome in this moment captured from the big day.

"I got your back, your front and sides 💗🎀🧜🏾‍♀️…..BIG 4🥺," Cardi captioned a series of photos from the party.

Milestone Moment

Kulture was an adorable grad — and Cardi was one proud mom — at the youngster's kindergarten graduation in 2023.

"My baby moving on up🎓I’m a emotional proud mommy🥺🖤Give me good grades and I’ll give you the world baby 🌏," Cardi wrote alongside photos from the day, which included a shot of Kulture — who wore a pink tiered dress under her blue graduation robe — embracing her mom.

Baby Face

Back in May, Offset showed some love for his youngest son as he shared photos of eight-month-old Wave on Instagram. The rapper wrapped his arms around the little guy as he looked at the camera dressed in a pint-sized outfit.

Bring Your Kid to Work Day

The proud parents had a special guest during a 2019 performance in New York as an adorable Kulture joined her parents on stage — pacifier and all!

All Together Now

While Cardi and Offset have two children, the Migos rapper is also father to three other children: sons Jordan and Kody and a daughter, Kalea Marie Cephus.

"It's a blessing to be able to have them all together, and to have a wife who's open and treats them as if they're her kids," Offset told Essence of their blended family. "It could be an issue, but I don't have to go through that, and that's beautiful."

Mama's Girl

To celebrate Teyana Taylor's 2020 album release, Cardi brought Kulture along for the ride in a yellow oversized bow and a coordinated rainbow outfit.

Welcome to the World

When announcing the birth of their second child together, Cardi shared this photo from her hospital bed on Instagram along with little Wave's birthday: "9/4/21."

In the photo, the former couple is seen looking down at their newborn son who is wrapped in a fluffy blue blanket.

Daddy's Little Girls

Offset had two very important girls by his side for the world premiere of The Little Mermaid in May 2023. Dressed like princesses themselves, the proud dad posed on the blue carpet with Kulture and Kalea.

"Mermaid premiere with my princesses," he wrote on Instagram alongside photos of their coordinated looks.

Mommy and Me

Big sister Kulture wrapped her arms around her little brother in this mirror selfie shared to X, formally known as Twitter, by the proud mom.

"All I need in this life of sin," she wrote alongside the adorable shot.

Matchy, Matchy

Cardi and Offset's little ones were joined by their aunt Hennessy Carolina in this sweet photo shared to Instagram — and check out their matching jewelry!
