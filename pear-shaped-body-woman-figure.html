Publish date: 2024-02-26

A WOMAN with a stunning pear-shaped figure has hit back at the trolls who call her body "weird".

Human bodies come in all shapes and sizes - triangle, apple, hourglass, rectangle and pear, the latter of which is how Grace Tutty would best describe hers.

The young online content creator, who is all about fashion and self-love, has an enviable waist with a wider pair of hips.

Combined, the two make her curves look like a pear, which Grace, from the UK, proudly shows off in figure-hugging dresses and outfits.

But whilst the stylish fashionista is all about body positivity and teaching others how to embrace their natural selves, her TikTok page, @gracetutty, has also attracted a number of trolls.

Recently, the dark-haired stunner found a comment left by a meanie who was puzzled by her body.
