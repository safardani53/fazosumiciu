Cleaning fanatics go wild for Lenor's 2.99 Exotic Bloom fabric softener saying it smells just like

Publish date: 2024-04-25

IF we had to narrow down our all-time favourite scents, Thierry Mugler's Alien perfume would feature pretty high on the list. After all, it's floral, it's woody and it lasts forever.

But just when we thought we couldn't get enough of the sweet aromas, fans of the cult favourite perfume have tracked down a new fabric softener which smells just like it.

And maybe now a bottle of Alien will last us longer than a couple months when we're busy dousing our clothes in Lenor's Exotic Blooms.

Posting on the Facebook group Extreme Couponing and Bargains UK, one happy shopper raved: "OMG they now have Lenor that smells like ALIEN perfume."

The classic perfume has amassed something of a cult following thanks to its rich amber scent and woody, jasmine top notes.

And it looks like Lenor have certainly been inspired by the fragrance - which has a floral base with notes of sweet amber.

  • Lenor Exotic Bloom, £2.99 from Home Bargains - buy now
  • Thierry Mugler Alien Perfume, £78 for 60ml from The Perfume Shop - buy now

And while a bottle of Alien will normally set you back the best part of £80, Lenor's fabric softener costs just £2.99 from Home Bargains in comparison - and it might just help your fragrance go a bit further.

What's more, this new release comes after Mrs Hinch fans went wild over Zoflora's £1.50 Midnight Bloom scent which they also claimed was a convincing dupe for Alien.

Needless to say, shoppers are now desperate to get their hands on a bottle of the softener - which lasts up to 52 washes.

Racking up over 2,000 comments, one user replied: "Whattttttttttt I need everything Alien."

Another added: "My next purchase!"
