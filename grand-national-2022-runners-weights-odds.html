Grand National runners: Weights and odds for every entry ahead of 2022 horse race at Aintree

Publish date: 2024-07-28

THE Grand National entries have been assigned their weights for the 2022 race at Aintree on April 9.

Some 104 horses have been entered into the world's most famous contest at Aintree.

But a maximum of 40 will run on the day, with a prize fund of £1million up for grabs and a worldwide audience.

Minella Times won last year under Rachael Blackmore, sealing a magical few weeks for trainer Henry de Bromhead and owner JP McManus.

That same horse is up 15lbs from last year, and near the top of the weights with dual winner Tiger Roll.

Gordon Elliott has already won the race three times - but will seek a hat-trick for his stable star in a bid to match Red Rum's record.

All the horses below are separated by their name, age, weight, official rating plus their owner and trainer. Odds are below.
