  • Select the sport you want to bet on using the left sidebar or top tabs.

    Sky Bet payment methods

    Sky Bet only accepts debit cards and Google or Apple Pay. While e-wallets like PayPal (and it still is for existing users), Neteller, and Skrill used to be accepted, new UK punters can only make accounts and add card payment methods. The accepted debit cards are Visa, Mastercard, Maestro, and Solo.

    Before you can deposit at Sky Bet, you must also verify your account. However, the verification is fast, using your debit card information to verify your identity.

    Credit cards aren’t available due to United Kingdom Gambling Commission (UKGC) regulations, and you can add up to three debit cards to your account. However, if you don’t want to use cards, then Apple Pay and Google Pay are also available on mobile, provided they’re not linked with a credit card.

    Sky Bet withdrawal options 

    When it comes to withdrawals, you have all the deposit methods available, as you must withdraw using a method you’ve deposited with. You’ll see a Net Amount listed next to each withdrawal method, which shows you how much you can withdraw using those payment methods.

    Sometimes, the UK bookmaker may need to verify your account further, especially if you’re requesting large withdrawals. This process will require you to provide copies of your identification, bank statement, proof of address, and other requested documents.

    The minimum withdrawal is £10, and the bookmaker processes the withdrawal request within two to five days. The only time the bookie processes it on the same day is for existing PayPal and other e-wallet users. There are no fees or maximum withdrawal limits, but the payment processor might have some.

    What is the minimum deposit  at Sky Bet?

    The minimum deposit is £5, but it’s £10 for existing UK PayPal users. Deposits are generally available in your account as soon as your bank authorizes it, meaning it’s near instant. The £5 minimum deposit is below the average, giving this bookmaker points in its favour when compared to other platforms.

    How to use cash out feature at Sky Bet

    The Cash Out feature allows you to withdraw your bet and possible winnings before a match is done. While it’s not available on all wagers, you’ll see a green arrow loop symbol to show when it’s possible. You can also look at your bet lists, and there’ll be a heading stating cash Out Bets, which lists all your wagers eligible for early cashout.

    Your cash-out value will be a calculation of the amount you bet, the price/odds at the time you placed the bet, and the current price/odds. However, the maximum cash-out amount is £500,000. Generally, you need to cash out while the team you bet on is still in the lead.

    There’s also the two-up promotion available to UK punters. This promotion means the bookmaker immediately settles you as the winner if your team leads by two goals. It’s an ongoing promotion on the platform you can make use of if you’re not sure whether the stake will be successful.
