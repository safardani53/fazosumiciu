Publish date: 2024-03-27

Orlando Henry "Tubby" Smith is an American college basketball coach. He is a men's basketball coach at High Point University, his alma mater. Smith has previously held similar roles at Tulsa University, University of Georgia, University of Kentucky, University of Minnesota, Texas University of Technology and University of Memphis. With Kentucky, he coached Wildcats to the 1998 NCAA championship. In his 28 years as head coach, Smith has had 26 championship seasons. In 2005, he joined Roy Williams, Nolan Richardson, Denny Crum and Jim Boeheim as the only head coach to win 365 games in 15 seasons or less. With the invitation of Texas Tech to the NCAA 2016, Smith became the second coach in history to lead five different teams at the NCAA. Smith's three sons followed in their father's footsteps as a coach.
