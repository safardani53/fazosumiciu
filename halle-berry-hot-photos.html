Publish date: 2024-05-18

Halle Berry is an Academy Award-winning actress known for her many roles in action-thrillers and beyond. If there’s one thing to know about Halle Berry, it’s that she can take on any role of any genre.

She burst onto the acting scene in the ’90s and landed major roles in ‘X-Men’ and ‘Die Another Day’ in the 2000s. The actress became known for her killer bod in the ‘007’ film after she showed off her fit physique in an orange and white bikini. She’s taken her talents all over the world, having filmed in exotic locations alongside Hollywood’s hottest hunks including Bruce Willis, Hugh Jackman, Keanu Reeves, Samuel L. Jackson, James Marsden, Alan Cumming, Michael Ealy and many more.Through the years, Halle has also become a fashion and red carpet icon.
