BARRY Manilow has been keeping a low profile after his shocking unrecognizable appearance at his Christmas show.

Barry, 80, was spotted getting out of his car in Palm Springs, California.

The Mandy singer kept a low-key, casual look during his outing. 

The Can’t Smile Without You hitmaker donned a grayish plaid shirt and dark trouser pants and paired them with black loafers. 

The music icon stepped out of his car and had his eyes covered with sunglasses.

His highlighted blondish hair appeared to be thinning.

THE CHRISTMAS SPECIAL

Barry's public appearance came after his Christmas special aired on NBC. 

On Monday, December 11, the 80-year-old star took the stage for an NBC special called A Very Barry Christmas.

The one-hour show was filmed at the Westgate Las Vegas Resort & Casino. 

In the behind-the-scenes preview, the singer teased: "It's filled with great Christmas songs. I've had three Christmas albums out, so I could have gone on for hours because they're wonderful.

'LOOK AT ME'

Rumors have swirled about Barry's changing appearance for years.

In November 2014, the veteran singer opened up about his looks and addressed the rumor mill on The Jonathan Ross Show.

Barry said: “Jonathan, look at me, don’t I look my age?...That’s the way I’m aging…”

He jokingly continued: “Really, this is what I look like. I don’t know what the hell they’re talking about.”

Barry added: “It’s infuriating. This is me at 70. It is. I swear.”

The singer also poked fun at himself: “My hips are not what they used to be.”

HIS PERSONAL LIFE

The former couple did not have any children during their short union.

Although Susan never remarried, the Copacabana star married his longtime manager, Garry Kief, in 2014.
