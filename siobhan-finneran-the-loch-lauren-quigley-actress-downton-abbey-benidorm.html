Who is Siobhan Finneran? Former Benidorm star who played Janice Garvey, and star of The Loch and Dow

Publish date: 2024-06-27

SIOBHAN Finneran is a well known face on the box, thanks to her long career on TV.

She is well known for her starring roles in Downton Abbey and Benidorm - but what else do we know about her?

Who is Siobhan Finneran?

Siobhan is a 51-year-old actress born in Lancashire.

She made her screen debut in the 1987 Independent film Rita, Sue and Bob Too.

Focusing her career on television, Siobhan has appeared in a host of well-loved TV shows.

Her most acclaimed role is that of Sarah O'Brien in period drama Downton Abbey, and appeared through to series three.

When was Siobhan in Benidorm?

Siobhan appeared in six series of Benidorm as the lovable Janice.

She starred in the show from series one in 2007 until 2015.

She will be back on TV TONIGHT (February 20, 2018) on Benidorm Ten Years On Holiday at 9pm to talk about her time on the show.

What else has Siobhan been in?

The actress appeared in the lotto-based drama The Syndicate and appeared in two series of Happy Valley

Siobhan also starred in the acclaimed The Moorside.

She recently starred in The Loch and Cold Feet.
