Publish date: 2024-06-21

THE biggest race in the horse racing calendar takes place TODAY at Aintree!

Jockeys, trainers and owners will all be eyeing up a slice of the tasty £1million prize pot on offer.

Last year's winner was an eye-opener as Noble Yeats, ridden by amateur jockey Sam Waley-Cohen triumphed and was priced at a huge 50-1.

Which just goes to show anybody can claim the prestigious trophy.

Winning the Grand National was a fairytale ending to Sam Waley-Cohen's career as a jockey.

When is the Grand National 2023?

  • The Grand National will take place TODAY, Saturday, April 15.
  • It will start at 5:15pm UK time.
  • Aintree Racecourse in Liverpool is the venue.

What channel is the Grand National 2023 on and can it be live streamed FREE?

  • The Grand National 2023 will be shown live on ITV and Racing TV.
  • You can stream all the action from Aintree for FREE via the ITVX app.
  • The ITVX app is available to download for FREE on your mobile or tablet device.
