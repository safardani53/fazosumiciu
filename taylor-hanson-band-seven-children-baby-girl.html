Who is Taylor Hanson and how many kids does he have?

Publish date: 2024-02-23

HANSON boy band member Taylor Hanson has seven children with his wife Natalie.

The most recent addition to their family was a baby girl born in December of 2020. 

Who is Taylor Hanson? 

Jordan Taylor Hanson, 37, who goes by the name Taylor, is an American singer and songwriter best known for being in a 1990’s boy band with his brothers.

Alongside his brothers Isaac, 39, and Zac, 34, he started the musical group as kids and were initially known as The Hanson Brothers.

The band later amended the name to Hanson. 

Their first single MMMBop made it to number one on the Billboard Hot 100 chart.

Taylor is also the lead singer of the group Tinted Windows.

How many kids does he have? 

Taylor and his wife Natalie Hanson have seven children together.

Little Maybellene Alma Joy Hanson was born on December 7, mom Natalie announced on Instagram.

The couple had announced on social media that they were expecting their seventh child. 

Taylor posted a picture and wrote: The best kind of unexpected. Number seven coming this December. #2020."

Natalie also shared a pic with Taylor in a field captioned: "Biggest little surprise in a long time. Baby number seven coming this December."

The new addition to the family joins siblings: Claude Indiana Emmanuel, 1, Wilhelmina "Willa" Jane, 8, Viggo Moriah, 11, Penelope "Penny" Anne, 15, River Samuel, 14, and Jordan Ezra Hanson, 17.

Who is his wife? 

Natalie Hanson, 36, is a contributing writer to Tulsa Kids Magazine and co-business owner of Modern Moniker with her sister-in-law.

She also has her own website called Nat on the Wall and blogs about her and her family’s travels.
