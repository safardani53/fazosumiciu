Princess Diana's astrologist reveals which star signs are most compatible (and those you should AVOI

Publish date: 2024-03-16

EVER wondered if you and your partner are really meant to be together? The answer could be written in the stars.

Princess Diana’s astrologer, Debbie Frank, has revealed which star signs are more compatible for relationships, and which will be a struggle – so how you do match up?

Debbie has revealed the ideal star sign matching in her new book, Written in the Stars.

In the introduction, she writes:  “I want to help you understand who the best people are for your sign, who to surround yourself with and who is on a different wavelength.”

She reveals how the star signs fit into four categories - Earth, Wind, Fire and Water – and some groups work better together than others.

Here are the signs that work best for mutual growth and support and which ones will be a challenge…

STAR SIGN CATEGORIES - FIRE, EARTH, AIR AND WATER

FIRE SIGNS 

  • Aries: 22 March - 21 April
  • Leo: 24 July - 23 August
  • Sagittarius: 23 November - 22 December

AIR SIGNS

  • Gemini: 23 May - 22 June
  • Libra: 24 September - 22 October
  • Aquarius:  21 January - 20 February

EARTH SIGNS

  • Taurus: 22 April - 22 May
  • Virgo: 24 August - 23 September
  • Capricorn: 23 December - 20 January

WATER SIGNS

  • Cancer: 23 June - 23 July
  • Scorpio: 23 October - 22 November
  • Pisces: 21 February - 21 March

Star signs that are most compatible

Debbie, who worked with Princess Diana from early 1989 until her death in 1997, revealed that the right elements can combine to support each other and create mutual growth.

She said that if your star signs are in the same group, you are in luck as you'll be moving through life with a kindred spirit.

The same group provides an immediate connection and desiring of similar things.

Earth signs (Taurus, Virgo and Capricorn) with Water signs (Cancer, Scorpio and Pisces)

According to Debbie, Earth and Water signs are both concerned with holding things together and making them real.

Their combined force means that they have all the tools to create an empire or build a relationship.

Earth is capable of providing the "glue" to make things stick, and the Water sign helps to be the creative outpouring that the Earth contains.

Together they form a vessel that carries the emotions in a relationship.

Fire signs (Aries, Leo and Sagittarius) with Air signs (Gemini, Libra and Aquarius)

These two signs are associated with outpouring, and also have extrovert, masculine and active energies.

Debbie says that this combination is when "doers meet the thinkers" and is the union of doing and talking.

Together you can make a glimmer of an idea into action and cause a force to be reckoned with.

Star signs that are least compatible

Don't feel discouraged if you and your partner aren't entirely compatible.

You may face certain challenges that will be hard work, but this can be exciting and help you to grow.

Just be aware that your partner may do things differently to you, and that is ok.

Air signs (Gemini, Libra and Aquarius) with Water signs (Cancer, Scorpio and Pisces)

Water signs want to connect, belong and understand, whereas Air signs are passionate about being free and spreading ideas.

Air can therefore feel held back by Water's desire to experience deep emotions, whereas Water can feel misunderstood by Air's rationality.

This pairing can be successful if both signs celebrate their differences and value what the other has to offer.

Fire signs (Aries, Leo and Sagittarius) with Water signs (Cancer, Scorpio and Pisces)

Where Water is happy to reflect, Fire is fuelled by wanting to do.

Either this can be challenge, or make for a creative combination.

If this pairing is to work, Fire must learn to not get impatient with Water's constantly changing emotions.

Fire can help to warm up Water, so just make sure it doesn't boil instead of simmering.

Air signs (Gemini, Libra and Aquarius) with Earth signs (Taurus, Virgo and Capricorn)

Air is desperate to occupy all places and fly, whereas Earth is happy to put down some secure roots.

Therefore a happy scenario can occur if each party is comfortable in their own role without trying to change the other.

Be careful for Earth to not clip the wings of Air, and for Earth to not feel continually uncomfortable at Air's desire to expand.

This pairing can work if Earth doesn't weigh Air down too much, and if Air lifts Earth up gently, without causing earthquakes.

Fire signs (Aries, Leo and Sagittarius) with Earth signs (Taurus, Virgo and Capricorn)

Fire is naturally rushing, whereas Earth is typically more cautious, so you have to be careful that Fire doesn't feel stifled or held back.

Fire can feel held back by the conservative nature of Earth, but Earth can feel annoyed at Fire's desire to constantly be on the move.

Either this can be a case of opposites attract if they combine their talents or would be better off not meeting at all.
