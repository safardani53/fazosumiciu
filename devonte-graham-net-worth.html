How Much Is Devonte’ Graham’s Net Worth?

As of 2023, Devonte’ Graham, who is 28 years old, has an estimated net worth of $10 million.

Currently, a point guard for the San Antonio Spurs, Graham, made most of his fortune from his basketball career.

His basketball passion turned into a profession after he was selected as the 34th overall pick in the 2018 NBA draft by the Atlanta Hawks. Immediately, he was traded to the Hornets, where his NBA journey began.

Earnings From Endorsements

Thanks to the NBA fame, he sees approaches for many endorsement deals.

Yet, the basketball star has not signed any significant sponsorship with sports giants.

But often on his social media posts, Devonte’ Graham features Nike’s Air Jordan, which he wears in the games too.

Some of his promotional deals on Instagram include Upside Shop (hat wear), boohooMAN (clothing store), SoleSavy (shoe store), and AMIRI (apparel).

As per calculation from Influencer Marketing Hub, his earnings per sponsored post range between $766- $1,277.

Moreover, he has appeared in some commercials for local businesses in Charlotte, such as Bojangles’ Famous Chicken ‘n Biscuits and OrthoCarolina.

NBA Contracts & Salary

On July 9, 2018, Devonte’ Graham signed a three-year contract worth $4.07 million with the Charlotte Hornets. 

After extending a qualifying offer of $4.74 million, the NBA player inked a four-year $47.3 million sign-and-trade contract with the Hornets on August 21, 2021. And immediately, he was sent to New Orleans Pelicans.

The deal has a $37.5 million guaranteed amount and an average annual salary of $11.825 million- a jump from a salary of $1.6 million in the 2020-21 season.

But after a season (2021-22 season) with the Pelicans, he was again traded to the San Antonio Spurs in the 2022-23 mid-season, on February 9, 2023.

As a result, both teams paid him a total of $11.55 million for the 2022 season; the Pelicans were liable to $7.63 million, while the Spurs paid him $3.91 million. That pay is 11.37% of the team’s cap.

It made his second-highest-paid player in the San Antonio Spurs of that season behind Doug McDermott with a base salary of $13.75 million.

High School & College

Graham attended Needham B. Broughton High School where he led the team to a 26–6 record and the 4A state championship game as a senior in the 2012-13 season.

Then, the player transferred to Brewster Academy in Wolfeboro, New Hampshire, where he teamed up with NBA rising star Donovan Mitchell.

For college basketball, he joined the University of Kansas & played for four seasons (from 2014 to 2018), averaging 12.4 points, 3.1 rebounds & 4.5 assists per game.

He graduated from the University with a degree in liberal arts and sciences.

NBA Draft & Professional Career

Devonte’ Graham was the 2018 NBA draft pick (34th overall) of the Atlanta Hawks before they traded him to the Charlotte Hornets.

With the Hornets, he played for three seasons, averaging 11.6 points and 4.5 assists per game.

Graham had a breakthrough season in 2019-20, improving his scoring to 18.2 points per game and finishing fifth in the Most Improved Player voting.

In 2021, the player signed a four-year contract with the New Orleans Pelicans in sign-and-trade deal. He played for the Pelicans for one season.

Graham also made a career-high nine three-pointers in a game against the Utah Jazz on December 27, 2021.

Later in February 2023, Graham was traded to the San Antonio Spurs in exchange for Josh Richardson.
