As the temperatures start to drop, it's time to break out the cozy hoodies. And what better way to show off your team spirit than with an NFL hoodie? With so many options, how do you choose the right hoodie for this season? Don't worry, because we're here to help. Read on for our guide on how to choose from top-quality NFL hoodies. 

Things To Consider Before Buying NFL Hoodies

Material

Some hoodies are made from cotton, while others are made from polyester or sherpa. So which material is right for you? 

Cotton is a natural fiber that is soft and breathable. It's ideal for wearing in mild weather but isn't as effective at insulating against cold temperatures.

Polyester is a synthetic fiber that is often used in athletic wear because it wicks moisture away from the body. It also dries quickly, making it a good choice for active wearers. 

Sherpa is a soft, brushed fabric typically used in outerwear because it is warm and cozy. It's perfect for chilly weather, but it usually too hot to wear in warm weather. 

In the end, the right material for you depends on your climate, personal preferences and needs. 

Stitching

When buying NFL hoodies, the stitching should be at the forefront of your mind. There are a few reasons for this. First, the hoodie's stitching quality will play a big role in how comfortable it is. If you choose a hoodie with a poorly designed hood, you'll likely find it uncomfortable to wear. 

The stitching will also impact its durability. A well-designed hoodie will be able to withstand wear and tear better than one that wasn't designed with durability in mind. 

Finally, the design can also affect its overall visual appeal. A well-designed hoodie will look great whether you wear it to the game or for a walk.

Additional Buying Guides

Size

There are many reasons to consider the size before buying an NFL hoodie for yourself. The most important reason is you’ll want it to fit well. If it is too small, it will be uncomfortable to wear. If it is too large, it will look odd and may not look great with the rest of your outfit. 

The best way to find the right size is to carefully follow the sizing chart. You can get a good idea of how accurate a size chart is by going through some customer testimonials on the product page. Then, you may go up or down a size depending on if you’re going for more of a fitted or oversized look. 

Color

When buying NFL hoodies, color is also an important consideration. First and foremost, some colors may be more associated with certain teams than others. If you want to show your support for a particular team, you’re obviously going to want to choose their colors. 

Also, certain colors may be more visible on the field than others. If you to make sure that you're easily seen in the stadium or spotted on the camera, consider opting for bright colors. 

How To Store NFL Hoodies?

NFL hoodies are a wardrobe staple for many football fans, but how can you make sure your hoodie stays in good condition over many seasons of the game? Here are some tips:

  • Avoid hanging your hoodie on a clothesline or hanger. This can cause the fabric to stretch out of shape.
  • Fold your hoodie carefully and store it in a drawer or closet. This will help protect the fabric from damage.
  • If you must wash your hoodie, use a gentle cycle and cool water. Allow the hoodie to air dry to prevent shrinkage.
  • If your hoodie has a stain, treat it immediately with mild detergent. Do not use bleach, as this can damage the fabric.
  • Never put your hoodie in the dryer. The heat can cause the fabric to shrink and damage the stitching.
