Kim Kardashian admits she REGRETS posing nude for 2007 Playboy photoshoot &amp; warns younger self 'don'

Publish date: 2024-07-20

KIM Kardashian has revealed she REGRETS posing nude for Playboy magazine in 2007 and warned her younger self to be "less trusting."

Going down the Keeping Up With The Kardashians memory lane in a bonus scene to celebrate the end of the show, Kim, 40, and her sisters were asked whether they'd like to "relive" or "if they regret" each one.

The Kardashian-Jenner clan had gathered together for the throwback segment of the bonus show, which comes as the iconic reality series draws to a close.

Kim, her sisters and mom Kris, 60, were asked whether they'd like to "relive" or if they "regret" each embarrassing moment.

"So the first one is, Kourtney waxing Khloe's vagina?" the voice off-screen could be heard asking, as Kourtney, 42, immediately shouted "relive".

Amongst the laughter, attentions quickly turned to Kim as the mystery voice asked, zooming in on her face: "Kim's playboy shoot?"

Not saying a word, the Skims creator scrunched her nose and shook her head as if to signify she "regretted" taking the 2007 magazine snaps.

"We're over it, regret, regret," her sisters could be heard saying off screen to encourage the team to quickly breeze over the awkward moment.

The interviewer then asked the clan for the one piece of advice each of them would give their younger selves when KUWTK had just started.

"I would say don't be so trusting," Kim answered with a solemn look. "But still keep your open heart and trust the universe."

Her sisters concurred, with Kendall adding she would say: "You have no idea. Just trust, and literally have faith in the universe."

Kourtney agreed: "That was mine, have faith in god and his plan for you," while Kris concurred: "I think trust the way things are going to work out for the best."

As they burst out into laughter, Kris then concluded: "I would tell my younger self to ditch some of the outfits. The crazy outfits I can't believe I wore."

Kim posed for a cover shoot with Playboy in 2007, with mom Kris overseeing the entire process and encouraging her from the sidelines.

At the time, Kim started off with lingerie and bathing suits before being stripped down completely as Kris told her: "You're doing amazing, sweetie."

Ex-husband Kanye made a thinly veiled jibe about the shoot long before their split in July 2020, as he wrote to Twitter his children would "never do Playboy".

Kanye also referred to the sex tape Kim made with her then-boyfriend Ray J in 2007, in a series of erratic tweets.

Referencing their daughter North, eight, he posted: "I put my life on the line for my children that North's mother would never sell her sex tape."

"I put my life on my God that North's mom would never photograph her doing Playboy and that's on God," he added in a follow-up tweet.

This week, mom Kim shared an emotional birthday message for her daughter North after Kanye West moved on with Irina Shayk.

The reality star wrote: "North, one day you will see these messages all printed out for you in the books I am making for you and so hope you feel the love because you bring so much love and joy into all of our lives!

"You are the silliest, most stylish, most creative person who knows exactly what they want in life! I’ve never met anyone like you!"

After six years of marriage, Kim filed for divorce from Kanye, 43, back in February, with the couple agreeing to split their $2billion fortune evenly

The hip-hop star filed for joint legal and physical custody of their four children, North, Saint, five, Chicago, three, and Psalm, two.

Kanye had been living at his Los Angeles ranch and church base while his romance with Irina, 35, who is also Bradley Cooper's ex, heated up.
