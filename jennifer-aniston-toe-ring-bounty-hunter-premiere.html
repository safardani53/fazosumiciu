Is It A Fresh Take On An Old Trend Or Just Terrible? Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-08-28

Do you have a go-to staple that you just can’t seem to part with? Well so does Jen!

Jennifer Aniston is a creature of habit — when she finds something she likes, she sticks to it! Take her affinity for black dresses, sexy strappy sandals, her staple hairdo, and finally her fetish for toe rings! When I saw Jen at the NYC premiere of The Bounty Hunter on March 16, I couldn’t get over how great she looked! It was so refreshing to see her in something aside from basic black, however after further inspection of Jen’s glam look from head to toe, I spotted Jen’s favorite accessory, a toe ring, on her middle toe! Jen, while I wish I never had to see that toe ring again, I can only hope next time you walk down the carpet you leave it at home!

Needless to say, I was never a toe ring fan, and the fact that she still wears one makes me cringe. Then again, many gals have certain accessories they aren’t willing to give up, regardless of how outdated they are (take thumb rings, certain piercings, and anklets, for example). It’s one thing to keep it on everyday, but is it the type of thing she should leave at home when she’s getting glam? Don’t get me wrong, it’s important for girls to wear what makes them feel comfortable and everyone should add some personal flair to their style choices, but what do you think? Does Jen’s toe ring add a personal touch to her overall look or should she ditch the bad accessory for good? What are some style staples that you refuse to part with, regardless of how un-trendy they may be?

-Katrina Mitzeliotis
