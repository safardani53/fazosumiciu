I got a tattoo as a dare on my inner thigh, people are always stunned when they realise what it says

Publish date: 2024-08-05

A WOMAN has left people stunned after she revealed what her inner thigh tattoo says.

Chloe Middleton, from Australia, shared a video of her getting the tattoo on TikTok, and it soon went viral.

In the video, Chloe revealed that she had the tattoo done as a dare - but fortunately Chloe loves the final result.

The tattoo is placed on her inner thigh and she used a dainty looking font for the text.

The social media influencer has over 70k followers on the app and has amassed over five million likes.

The font makes the text difficult to read, but people were left stunned when she revealed what the inking said.

In another TikTok video, she replied to a comment asking what the tattoo said.

And it seems the young woman had taken inspiration from the notorious Cardi B song WAP.

She revealed: "It says - bring a bucket and a mop," as she continued to sing the song.

Chloe added: "I just think it's so f***ing funny because it looks so like delicate and beautiful with like this nice little handwriting and it literally says bring a bucket and a mop."
