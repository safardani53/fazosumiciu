Rosie O'Donnell, 61, shoves Steve Harvey aside and bursts into song in abrupt Celebrity Family Feud

Publish date: 2024-04-08

ROSIE O'Donnell has managed to steal the spotlight from host Steve Harvey on Wednesday's Celebrity Family Feud episode.

The View alum pushed Steve out of the way to give herself some room when she belted a rendition of a holiday classic in the middle of the game.

Rosie, and her family, competed against Pose star Billy Porter, and his family before she paused midway for an impromptu performance.

The 61-year-old actress walked up to Steve, 66, and said: “Excuse me? Steve? I don’t know if you realize I’ve been on Broadway singing three times, a musical, I was with Billy Porter.

“I don’t have Tonys, I don’t have Grammys,” she said as she took to the front of the stage.

Rosie then nudged Steve to the side so she could be front and center.

Brimming with the holiday spirit, Rosie confidently sang her version of Frosty the Snowman, with the audience joining in on the festive fun.

As the classic tune reached the iconic "made out of coal" line, she playfully shouted "COAL" directly at a startled Steve, who found himself caught in the whirlwind of Rosie's festive fervor.

But Rosie wasn't the only one spreading holiday cheer on the set.

In a heartwarming moment, Billy, 54, also took the stage to perform a holiday song, turning the Celebrity Family Feud into a festive fiesta of singing.

SPEECHLESS STEVE

Steve has been stunned more times than once on Celebrity Family Feud.

In July, contestant Becky Lynch, 36, stunned the game show host when she suggested he hook up with Kim Kardashian, 43.

The episode featured WWE male superstars versus their female counterparts.

The men's team included such favorites as captain Seth Rollins, Big E, Kofi Kingston, Xavier Woods, and Dolph Ziggler.

While the women were fronted by Seth's real life wife, Becky.

She was joined by Liv Morgan, Alexa Bliss, Bayley, and Natalya.

But host Steve was the center of attention for one question.

"If Steve Harvey wasn't married," the TV star asked, "what female celebrity would you match him up with?"

Lucky ladies like Beyonce and Halle Berry each made one of the top five answers, but when the men reached three strikes, Becky dropped a bombshell suggestion.

"Kim Kardashian?" she shyly said.

This seemed to take Steve by surprise, as he stumbled and stuttered over his response.

"Wow… heh, whoo… that’s interesting," he said, making clearly uncomfortable facial expressions.

The big board seemed to agree with him, as Kim's name wasn't up there, and the men stole the points.

Fans online pondered the match as well, with one person even directly tweeting to The Kardashians star.

"So, Steve Harvey?" the user asked Kim; however the Hulu star has yet to reply.

In the end, the men came from behind to win.
