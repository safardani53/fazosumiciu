Who Is Christine Brown's Husband? All About David Woolley

Publish date: 2024-10-01

Christine Brown is married!

The Sister Wives star and David Woolley wed in October 2023 after announcing their engagement in April of that same year.

Prior to meeting Woolley, Brown was "spiritually married" to Kody Brown for 25 years. They eventually split in November 2021.

"I want a partnership. I want a guy who actually loves me and wants to be with me intimately," Brown told PEOPLE in August 2022. "I will be a monogamist from here on out. Polygamy is not for me anymore."

Brown and Woolley made their relationship public in February 2023 when they posted about each other on Instagram.

"I finally found the love of my life, David. The first time he held me close, it felt like my soul took its first breath," the reality star wrote alongside a sweet selfie of the couple.

So, who is Christine Brown's husband? Here's everything to know about David Woolley and his relationship with the TLC star.

He and Brown publicized their relationship on Valentine's Day in 2023

Brown hard launched her romance with Woolley in a sappy Valentine's Day post on Instagram.

"I finally found the love of my life, David," she wrote. "The first time he held me close, it felt like my soul took its first breath. He's wonderful and kind, incredible with my children and an adorable grandpa. I never dreamed I could find a love like this."

A week prior, the Sister Wives alum teased that she was dating someone "exclusively."

"He's wonderful and romantic and so kind, and everything I've been looking for," Brown said on her Instagram Story.

He proposed to Brown after a few months of dating

Woolley asked Brown to marry him in April 2023 while on a weekend getaway in Utah.

"David treats me like a queen and tells me I'm beautiful every day," she told PEOPLE of her now-husband. "I've never been in love like this before, and the world seems like a brighter place with him in it. I'm so excited for the wonderful adventure we are going to embark on for the rest of our lives."

Brown also shared their engagement news with fans on Instagram. "We're engaged!" she captioned a sweet photo of her and Woolley. "David popped the question VERY romantically, and I said YES! I'm so excited and just live in bliss every day!"

Woolley proposed with a unique diamond ring, which featured three bands intertwined — one silver, one gold and one black. Brown showed off her new bling on Instagram and wrote, "We don't always get second chances in life, I'm blessed to have found mine. To have found my happily ever after. It's time to plan a wedding!!!"

He and Brown got married in October 2023

The couple tied the knot on Oct. 7, 2023, in Moab, Utah. Roughly 300 guests were in attendance and Brown's former sister wife Janelle Brown was part of the bridal party.

Ahead of the "big white wedding," Woolley and Brown spoke with PEOPLE.

"She wants to be able to wear a white dress and walk down the aisle with her dad,” the groom said. “She never got to do that [for] her first marriage. She wants all the bells and whistles.”

Brown added: “It's a fairytale. It's everything I never thought I would have. I get to walk down the aisle to David, him just looking at me the way that he does, it’s a dream come true.”

He's a proud grandpa

Woolley has only been on Instagram since February 2023, but his page is adorned with cute family photos of his adventures with his daughter Kati's three young sons.

"Grandkids are the best," Woolly captioned a photo carousel of him with his grandchildren. One snap is of Woolly and his grandchild relaxing on the couch in their pajamas, while Woolly poses for a picture with his grandchild in front of a fountain in another. In the last photo, the grandad is seen gazing at his child's newborn warmly swaddled in a hospital blanket.

The couple have already begun blending their families. In April, Woolley shared a photo of him, Brown and Truely visiting Kati and her family. "Had so much fun seeing my daughter and her boys for a short visit!" he wrote alongside the family portrait.

He gets along with Brown's daughter, Truely

While Woolley and Brown are avid adventurers themselves, Woolley has also started "making memories" and exploring other parts of the world alongside his soon-to-be stepdaughter, Truely. In April 2023, the trio embarked on a family vacation to Los Angeles, where they visited popular tourist destinations like Universal Studios, Voodoo Doughnuts and the beach.

"Making memories and going through adventures have always been precious to me. I've been blessed enough with the ability and time to make these core memories with my kids," Brown captioned a photo carousel, including multiple photos of the three of them at the Wizarding World of Harry Potter.

Woolley, Brown and Truely also stopped to snap a selfie with their giant donuts, while another photo shows the three of them relaxing at the beach in the sunshine.

Brown added, "Adventuring with @david__woolley and Truely in California @unistudios this weekend has been wonderful."

His nickname for Brown is "queen"

These lovebirds have a thing for nicknames! Woolley often refers to Brown as his "queen" and he her "king" on social media. The day they went Instagram official, Woolley posted a solo shot of his queen and expressed how he's "the luckiest guy."

"When I'm with you, we just sit in our own little bubble and the world can go around us and we're just standing still and it's perfect and I have never felt like this before. When I took you to the The Little Mermaid play I saw how much you loved life and I knew I had some one special for me," he wrote. "I'm the luckiest guy, thank you for finding me. Love, your king ❤️"

In March 2023, Woolley uploaded a photo of a mirror on which Brown had written, "I love you! My King." He then thanked his "soulmate" and "love" for "all the little things she does for me."

Brown frequently calls her now-husband "king" in her own Instagram posts or with a hashtag.
