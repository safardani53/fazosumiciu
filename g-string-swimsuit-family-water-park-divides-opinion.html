Swimmer slammed for wearing an 'inappropriate' G-string bikini at family water park but not everyone

Publish date: 2024-09-07

A SWIMMER was slammed after she wore a tiny G-string swimsuit to a family water park with some branding her outfit "inappropriate."

Footage showing the woman nearly naked in the flesh-coloured one-piece surrounded by splashing kids has divided opinion online with users saying there is nothing wrong with her swimsuit.

A concerned parent who filed the video, zoomed in to show the woman's costume and shared it on TikTok.

Only a sliver of fabric is visible when she is facing away from the camera.

The text overlay on the clip asks: “Is this appropriate for a family water park or NOT?”

The TikToker who posted it asked others to weigh in on the debate, while making her own view clear.
