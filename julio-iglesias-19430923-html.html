Julio Iglesias (World Music Singer) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Publish date: 2024-07-05

Julio José Iglesias de la Cueva is a Spanish professional soccer singer, songwriter and former soccer player. Iglesias is recognized as the world's most widely marketed continental European singer and one of the best sellers in music history, having sold over 100 million records worldwide in 14 languages. It is estimated that during his career he performed in more than 5,000 concerts, for more than 60 million people on five continents. In April 2013, Iglesias was inducted into the Latin Composers Hall of Fame. In 1983 Iglesias was named the man who recorded the songs in the most languages ​​in the world, and in 2013 to be the Latin artist with the best-selling records in history. In April 2013 in Beijing, he was honored as the most popular international artist in China. In Brazil, France, Italy and elsewhere, Iglesias was the biggest seller of foreign records, while in his home country, Spain, he sold the most records in history, with 23 million discs.
