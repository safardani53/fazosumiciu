Angela Kinsey Photos Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-09-15

Angela Kinsey, born June 25, 1971, is an American actress. Kinsey rose to fame on the NBC ensemble comedy series ‘The Office,’ for which she earned two Screen Actors Guild Awards for her work with the cast. She played the role of Angela Martin from 2005-2013, appearing in over 180 episodes. 

Since her time on ‘The Office,’ the actress has appeared in shows like ‘Haters Back Off!,’ ‘Never Have I Ever,’ ‘New Girl,’ and more. She is also the host of the podcast ‘Office Ladies,’ with her former ‘Office’ co-star and real life best friend, Jenna Fischer. She is married and has a daughter and two step-sons.
