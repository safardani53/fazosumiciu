Delta Goodrem beats Margot Robbie to top Maxims Hot 100 Australia list

Publish date: 2024-05-10

DELTA Goodrem scrubs up all white in a low-cut dress — much to the frustration of fellow Aussie Margot Robbie’s male fan club.

Singer Delta, 35, was named No1 on men’s mag Maxim’s Hot 100 Australia list of 2020 — ahead of actress Margot, 30.

Delta said: “Standing alongside so many beautiful women feels very special.”

No surprise her guitarist boyfriend Matthew Copley says he’s “the No1 luckiest guy”. 

The lads' mag features the world's sexiest woman on its cover every year.

Former Miss Universe winner Olivia Culpo, who is dating NFL star Christian McCaffrey, topped the 2019 Maxim Hot 100 poll.

In 2018, the magazine named Kate Upton as the sexiest woman in the world.

Kate succeeded 2017's winner Hailey Baldwin to the coveted title.
