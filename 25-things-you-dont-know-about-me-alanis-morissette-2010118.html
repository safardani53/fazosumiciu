25 Things You Don't Know About Me: Alanis Morissette

Publish date: 2024-07-20

Alanis Morissette, 36, (who will return as a guest star on Weeds Aug. 16) shares the 25 things you don't know about her with UsMagazine.com.

1. I speak French and English, and I spoke German while I was a child living in Germany.

2. I have a debilitating fear of moths.

3. I'm in the middle of writing a book that is part psychology, part philosophy, part spirituality, part Q&A, part prose, part art…and is not a memoir.

4. I am a huge fan of my twin brother, Wade Morissette’s kirtan music.

5. I love historical and autobiographical movies.

6. The smartest thing I have ever done is marry my husband, [rapper] Mario Treadway.

7. I love designing homes and spaces.

8. During my travels, I like to collect essential oils from around the world.

9. Public recognition embarrasses me.

10. I love being on airplanes.

11. I make a mean chocolate-chip banana bread.

12. I love red wine.

13. I love cutting people's hair.

14. I am half Hungarian.

15. My favorite food is  [Japanese hot pot] shabu shabu.

16. I have three turtles, and they love to pile up on each other.

17. I am profoundly sensitive.

18. I am a bona fide jock.

19. I fancy myself a proficient massage therapist.

20. I love getting older.

21. My favorite place in the world is California’s Big Sur.

22. I live for my rescue dogs.

23. I have never had a cavity.
