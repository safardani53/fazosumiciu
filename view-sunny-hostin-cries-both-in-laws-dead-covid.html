The View's Sunny Hostin cries as she reveals both of her in-laws died from Covid-19 and begs viewers

Publish date: 2024-06-24

SUNNY Hostin broke down in tears as she announced on Friday’s episode of The View that both of her in-law’s have died from Covid-19. 

The talk show host revealed that her husband, Emmanuel Hostin, lost his mother and father - who were both physicians - to coronavirus within days of each other. 

In an emotional flee on today’s episode of the ABC daytime series, Sunny looked somber as she began to open up about her family’s tragic month. 

“After speaking with my husband, he felt that from a public health standpoint it was very important for me to share that we are deeply saddened that Manny lost both of his parents over the holidays,” she began holding back tears. 

As her voice trembled, she revealed: “He lost his father on Dec. 28 and he lost his mother on New Year’s Day, both to COVID.”

She continued: “They were both physicians and they were both very careful. They didn’t celebrate Thanksgiving with us because we all decided it wasn’t safe.

“And after a lot of contact tracing, we still don’t know how they contracted this virus.”

“COVID is very, very serious,” she said. “It is not a joke, it is not a hoax,” she nearly yelled with a stern tone. 

Sunny’s in-laws were reputable medical experts Dr. Maria Jesus, 78, and Dr. A. Emmanuel - who died days before his 83rd birthday. 

The couple is survived by their children, Emmanuel, Helen, Carlos as well as their three grandchildren.

The attorney and television personality went on to thank her co-hosts for their support and revealed b Whoopi Goldberg sent food while Meghan McCain sent her a bottle of bourbon.

She concluded her tearful speech, begging: “We want everyone to know that you may think you’re healthy and you won’t be impacted, but you may be an asymptomatic carrier. 

“You could put someone you love at risk. So please, I beg of you, social distance, wear your mask, wash your hands, and if you get the opportunity to take this vaccine, take it.”

Sunny has been married to orthopedic surgeon Emmanuel Hostin since 1998. 

Shortly after their show aired, Meghan, 36, tweeted support for her friend and cast mate.

“My heart breaks for you @sunny and the entire Hostin family. Covid is a true, unadulterated evil,” the daughter of late Arizona Senator John McCain wrote. 

Yesterday, the United States set a new record for singly-day death tolls which is now close to 4,000. 

According to John Hopkins University - where Emmanuel studied medicine - the country has confirmed over 21,500,000 cases of coronavirus with over 365,000 deaths as of Thursday.
