Jade Roper Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-08-10

Bio:
Jade Roper (born Jade Elizabeth Roper in Gering, Nebraska, on December 18, 1986) is a contestant on season 19 of The Bachelor, fighting for Chris Soules. She worked in LA as a cosmetic developer. She makes it to the final four, but Chris finds out that she has a secret past — she posed nude in Playboy magazine. She comes off very shy so it’s quite the shock to the girls and to Chris. She competed on the second season of Bachelor in Paradise. In the season finale episode, she got engaged to Tanner Tolbert.

Best Known For:
Jade Roper is best known for competing for Chris’ heart on The Bachelor, as well as competing on Bachelor in Paradise.
