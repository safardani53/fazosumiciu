B&amp;M is selling thermal leggings for a fiver and shoppers say its like wearing a teddy bear

Publish date: 2024-09-25

WINTER would be great if we could stay cocooned in cosy pyjamas all day.

Unfortunately, we do need to leave the house from time to time - so it's just as well B&M are selling a pair of thermal leggings that shoppers can't wait to get their hands on.

Posting on the Facebook group 'Extreme Couponing and Bargains UK', a woman called Tammy revealed how she had come across the super-warm product called 'Thermal Brushed Leggings' in the popular bargain store.

Captioning images of the packaging and the fluffy lining, she wrote: "Thermal leggings from B&M.

"Omg they are amazing!! So warm I can't believe they are only a fiver. 

"Second pic is the inside so you can see the material, it's like wearing the teddy bear material.

"They do come up small I think. I'm a 12-14 and the L-XL is a good fit."

Her recommendation - priced at £4.99 and which is described as having a "super soft plush lining", was popular with fellow member - and the post quickly received more than 280 'likes'.
