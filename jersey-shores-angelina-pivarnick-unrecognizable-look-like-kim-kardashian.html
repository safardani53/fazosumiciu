Jersey Shore's Angelina Pivarnick is unrecognizable in new pics after fans say she's trying to look

Publish date: 2024-08-24

JERSEY Shore's Angelina Pivarnick looked unrecognizable in new photos after fans accused her of altering her appearance to look like Kim Kardashian.

Fans slammed the 35-year-old earlier this month for "photoshopping herself" so much that she looked like one of the Kardashian sisters.

Angelina shared videos to her Instagram Stories of her in full glam after a session with her hair and makeup team.

The MTV star then went on to post a photo showcasing the look causing fans again to point out the resemblance between her and Kim.

In the pic, Angelina donned a full face of makeup and hair extensions pulling her long dark hair into a half up ponytail.

She let the front two strands of her hair rest down along her face as she gazed at the camera.

She captioned the post: "Monday glam for my halloween themed photoshoot : Pics coming SOON ! Mua," tagging her glam squad.

The comment train continued with fans calling her out for copying the model's look.

One user wrote: "Kim Kardashian 2.0," while another agreed replying: "Kim Kardashians twin"

'KARDASHIAN TERRITORY'

Earlier this month, the Jersey Shore Family Vacation star was accused by fans for being a Kardashian wannabee after she posted a photo of her in a body-hugging black dress, with a V-neck showing off ample cleavage.

She kept her hair in a half-up, half-down look and had her face completely made up with smokey eyes, dark brows, glossy lips, and rosy cheeks.

Jersey Shore fans on Reddit said she looked great but slammed her for using so much “photoshop” to make her look that way, saying she’s getting into “Kardashian territory.”

One fan said she’s “not fooling anyone” because they see her “on tv every week” on MTV.

Another wrote: “It's just so …extra. I mean we see her on tv and she looks good so what's the pt of all the extra editing? 

“I'm over this ‘edit me into a Kardashian' wave"

A third slammed the photo, commenting: “She looks nothing like her original self. 

“She’s morphing into Kim Kardashian who also looks nothing like her original self. It’s bizarre.”

Others said they “thought this was a kardashian until” they looked at Angelina’s name and this is probably “90% photoshop.”

HER TRANSFORMATION

Angelina has continued to transform her appearance since viewers were introduced to the Staten Island native.

The reality star recently admitted to getting plastic surgery including a boob job, a butt lift, and liposuction.

She previously explained in a show confessional: "... I also never ever got a nose job. Same nose since I was born. So nice try on that. I just contour it now with makeup!"

"MY ONLY SURGERY HAS BEEN MY BOOBS! Ask my plastic surgeon. He knows. I’ve gotten injections and that’s it!"

However, Angelina later decided to get a butt lift and lipo as well, as she live-streamed the process.

Back in July, the star also shared she underwent a fat-burning procedure on her stomach saying it was "worth the hype" and she's had "amazing results."

Angelina wrote over another snap: "So far amazing results. Today is session #4, and my tummy is flatter!!! I even see crazy definition. More pics to follow when I get home."
