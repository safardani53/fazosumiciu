A WOMAN has revealed a clever way to avoid paying £7 for the Flash mop refills.

We all want to find ways to save money where we can as the cost of living crisis continues, so her tip couldn't come at a better time.

Georgia Kendall, from the UK, posted the hack to her TikTok account where it soon went viral with over 60k views.

In the video, she said: "I have uncovered a life hack and I'm quite proud of myself because, to be honest, I'm thick as s***."

She revealed she would always purchase the Flash speed mop refills, which cost £7 for 24 sheets.

But she wondered if a pack of regular floor wipes would fit on the mop.

She used the Asda apple scented floor wipes which are £1 for 15 wipes.

Georgia placed the wipe on the floor and then tucked the corners into the flash mop, which fit like a glove.

She then demonstrated how well the hack worked as she cleaned her kitchen floor.

"The cleaning hack you didn’t know you needed," she captioned the post.
