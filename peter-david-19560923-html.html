Peter Allen David, often abbreviated PAD, is an American writer of comics, novels, television, films and video games. His notable comic book works include 12-year award-winning work on The Incredible Hulk, as well as Aquaman, Young Justice, Supergirl, Fallen Angel, Spider-Man, Spider-Man 2099, and X-Factor. His Star Trek works include comics, novels like Imzadi, and co-creators of the New Frontier series. His other novels include film adaptations, media links, and original works, such as the series Apropos of Nothing and Knight Life. His TV productions include shows like Babylon 5, Young Justice, Ben 10: Alien Force and Nickelodeon's Space Case, which he co-created with Bill Mumy. David often jokes about his profession as "Writer of Stuff", and is known for his prolific writing abilities, characterized by a mix of real-world problems with humor and greedy material. popular culture references, as well as super-fiction and self-referencing elements.
