Nick, Nate, Elizabeth, And Alexandra

Publish date: 2024-06-29

Joe Montana, the ‘Joe Cool’ has been married three times to three different women, however, he has four kids with his recent wife Jennifer Wallace.

His eldest daughter Alexandra is a lawyer, the second born Elizabeth is into modeling. Moreover, Montana’s two sons Nathaniel and Nicholas, like his father are into sports.

The former NFL quarterback is proud of all of his children, who have all grown up, moved in with their own families, and succeeded in their careers.

Joseph Clifford Montana Jr. also known as ‘Joe Cool’ and ‘the Comeback Kid’ is an American former football quarterback who played in the NFL for 16 seasons.

Widely regarded as one of the greatest quarterbacks of all time, Montana primarily played most of his career with the San Francisco 49ers.

The eight-time Pro Bowl and 3 times Super Bowl MVP also led San Francisco to four Super Bowl Championships.

Moreover, the Pro Football Hall of Famer made it into the First-team All-Pro three times and has two NFL Most Valuable Player of the Year with one NFL Comeback Player of the Year.

After his playing career came to an end in 1994, San Francisco retired No. 16 Jersey in honor of his incredible playing career.

Joe Montana Kids

Before Montana’s marriage to Jennifer, he was previously married to two other women, with whom he has no kids.

However, Jennifer Montana and Joe have four kids, all of whom are doing well in their separate fields of work. Of his 4 children, Joe already has a granddaughter with his first child Alexandra Montana.

Daughters Alexandra & Elizabeth

Joe and Jennifer welcomed their first child on October 10, 1985. The eldest daughter Alexandra is currently 38 years old.

Alexandra, a lawyer by occupation, attended the University of Notre Dame to study law in 2004.

Moreover, she furthered her studies in 2008 at Loyola Marymount University pursuing a Master’s and doctorate of Law.

In 2020, she caught the media’s eye when someone tried to take her child from their home in Malibu, California.

The intruder’s plan was foiled by Jennifer and Joe Montana, who were there and made it tough for them to snatch the child.

Unlike Joe’s eldest daughter, the second-born Elizabeth Jean Montana is a model.

Following in the footsteps of her mom, Elizabeth pursued modeling from an early age. She describes herself as a fashion addict who started in elementary school.

Moreover, the 36-year-old is also an actress best known for the 2013 movie ‘Hey Mickey.’

More On Children Nate & Nick

The elder son of Joe, Nathaniel Joseph Montana is a former American football quarterback who followed in his father’s footsteps.

Nate was a reserve quarterback at De La Salle High School in California.

Later, he walked on at Notre Dame as a freshman in 2008 before transferring to Pasadena City College in 2009.

After re-enrolling at Notre Dame the next year, Montana transferred to the University of Montana within a month.

Later, he played for West Virginia Wesleyan in NCAA Division II program in 2012.

However, the prospective football player got undrafted in the 2013 NFL Draft and so ended his playing career.

Like his elder brother, the other kid of Joe Montana, Nick is also a former football quarterback.

Nicholas Alexander Montana began his college football at the University of Washington in 2009. Later, he transferred to Mt. San Antonio College in 2012.

After a year, Nick joined Tulane University where he played for two seasons and retired from professional football in 2015 after failing the 2015 NFL Draft.
