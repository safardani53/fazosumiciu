Singles Inferno fans shocked as they recognize Season 3 cast member from another popular Netflix

Publish date: 2024-06-25

SEASON 3 of Single’s Inferno has only just begun on Netflix, but fans have already made a shocking discovery about one of the contestants.

Eagle-eyed viewers recognized cast member Yun Ha-bin from another one of the streamer’s popular shows.

The first three episodes of the new season of Single’s Inferno were released on Netflix on Tuesday.

Fans were introduced to the new cast of the Korean reality dating series, which included Ha-bin.

Per the rules of the show, the contestants aren’t allowed to reveal their ages nor their careers while on the “inferno,” aka the deserted island where they're stationed.

Once they match up with another contestant by choosing each other as the one they like the most, they can head to “paradise,” aka a nice night at a hotel, where they can divulge their personal details.

Not all of the contestants made it to paradise during the first three episodes, and Ha-bin was one of those unlucky few.

While he hasn’t yet been able to reveal his profession on the reality show, many fans have already discovered this information.

For one, while his Instagram only features a few photos, his bio reveals that he’s an actor.

Not only is he an actor, but he’s actually appeared on another Netflix show – and recently at that.

The Korean romantic drama My Demon began on the streaming service in late November, just a couple of weeks before Single’s Inferno Season 3 started.

Ha-bin appeared in episode 2 as Noh Soo-an’s secretary, and fans quickly recognized him from the role and began sharing his scene on social media.

Other viewers chimed in on the conversation, either also recognizing him from the drama or at least knowing they’d seen him before.

One social media user wrote: “OMG I NOTICED HIM IN MY DEMON!”

A second commented: “Ohhh I had the same thought the first time I saw him.”

Another insisted: “I knew he looked familiar.”

A fourth fan wondered: “HOW DIDNT I NOTICE THIS I WATCHED BOTH SHOWS RECENTLY”

“No wonder he looked familiar the moment I saw him omg,” wrote a fifth.

Aside from his guest role on My Demon, Ha-bin has also appeared in a few other projects over the last few years.

Earlier in 2023, he held a main role in a six-episode youth drama called Our D-Day, according to MyDramaList.

The TV personality was also in a 2019 action movie, Bongcheon Tiger Lee, and a thriller the year before, Fallen.
