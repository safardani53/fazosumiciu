Carrie Johnson shows off baby bump in new online post before scotching speculation she's pregnan

Publish date: 2024-02-10

CARRIE Johnson shows off her baby bump in an online post yesterday, before scotching speculation she is pregnant again.

The shot was taken in June just before Frankie, her third child with ex-PM Boris, was born.

Carrie, 35, said she clarified the date “just in case you thought I might be pregnant again”.

The media consultant added that she would be spending her New Year’s “with a Chinese takeaway on the sofa”.

The couple confirmed their relationship when she supported Boris' leadership bid in June 2019. 

They later married on May 29, 2021, at Westminster Cathedral only 1.5 miles away from their then-home in Downing Street.
